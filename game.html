▸ BREAKING: PIPE MISALIGNED, LEDGER UPDATED (AGAIN)
The Undertaking confirms that a minor discrepancy of uncertain origin occurred somewhere between Intake Valve C, the Auxiliary Ration Cupboard, and a page of the Ledger that everyone agrees was definitely blank yesterday. No injuries have been recorded, several hats were misplaced, and the situation has been formally reclassified from Concerning to Administrative. Residents are advised to continue their duties as normal, unless their duties involve asking questions, in which case alternative duties will be assigned.
According to sources who are authorised to know things, the issue was resolved promptly once it was written down, stamped twice, and read aloud to a room containing at least one witness and a kettle. The resulting paperwork now exists in triplicate, none of which agree with each other, which is considered a healthy outcome. A spokesperson noted that "systems are functioning within expected tolerances," before adding that expectations had recently been adjusted to be more realistic.
The Daily Undertaking reminds readers that order is not the absence of chaos, but the proper filing of it. Any rumours suggesting pipes have opinions, or that the Ledger occasionally sighs, are unhelpful and probably the result of fatigue. Further updates will be issued as soon as they are approved, countersigned, misplaced, rediscovered, and quietly celebrated as a success.
